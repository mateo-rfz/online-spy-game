Welcome to the Spy Game!

This is a game where players have to guess who the spy is among them. The spy doesn't know who the other players are, and the players try to find the spy.

Game Features:

  • Multiple players can join.
  • Each player gets a role: either a normal player or a spy.
  • The goal is for the players to guess who the spy is or for the spy to stay hidden.
  • Roles are assigned randomly at the start of the game.
